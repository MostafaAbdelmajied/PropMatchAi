The Problem

Rental is expensive, slow, and hard to trust.

Long-term rentals still depend on brokers, repeated calls, unclear ownership, and manual paperwork.

💸

High broker commissions

Tenants pay a large fee just to reach property owners.

⚠️

Fake or duplicated listings

Many listings are repeated, outdated, or not verified.

📞

Repeated calls

Tenants and owners keep answering the same questions.

🤝

Low trust

Both sides lack clear proof before meeting or paying.

📄

Manual contracts

The rental process is still fragmented and unstructured.

Cost Model (1/3)

AI and verification operating costs

A) OCR verification cost

  • 200 owners
  • 2 documents per owner
  • OCR per document: 3-7 EGP (average 5 EGP)
  • 200 × 2 × 5 = 2,000 EGP
  • Estimated OCR cost: 2,000 EGP

B) Owner RAG chat cost

  • 200 listings
  • 30 AI replies/listing/month = 6,000 replies
  • Cost per reply: 0.5-1 EGP (average 0.75 EGP)
  • 6,000 × 0.75 = 4,500 EGP
  • Estimated owner AI chat cost: 4,500 EGP
